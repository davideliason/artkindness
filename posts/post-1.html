Why Art and Kindness Belong Together

Art can be beautiful, inspiring. It an lead to self-contemplating, the considering of new ideas, perspectives and paradigms. It can change the life of an individual and even a community. It can be a catalyst for change and connection. Art can also be the wrapper for action or creations that have a functional purpose. It serves as a bridge of praxis.

Without a doubt, art is important for how it can inspire, challenge perceptions, and invite deeper conversations. That layer is really important, and offers so much depth in and of itself. To me, it's really interesting to look at adding a layer of praxis, of adding some practical usage as well. Art being functional as well.

The foundation of creating such art of praxis is starting with the intention of kindness- wanting to create something that will be kind to people in its effects. That being the primary motivator, instead of monetary gain. That, I believe, makes all the difference.
